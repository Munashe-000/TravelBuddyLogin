Cape Town


Cape town is a beautiful city located in the Wester Cape province of South Africa. Often referred to as the "Mother City", it offers a premium tourist experience for travellers looking to enjoy the the coastal areas of the country. It is also home to one of the seven wonders of the world, Table Mountain. It boasts a number of other tourist attractions such a lovely beach resorts and the infamous Robben Island where humanitarian icon Nelson Mandela was held prisoner.
